Easy Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Salt and pepper, to taste
8 ounces fettuccine pasta
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
Chopped parsley, for garnish
Instructions:

Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Cook in a skillet until no longer pink. Set aside and slice.

Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

In the same skillet, melt butter and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.

Add heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir until smooth and creamy.

Combine cooked pasta, sliced chicken, and the creamy sauce.

Serve, garnished with chopped parsley.

This version retains the essence of Chicken Alfredo but with fewer steps. Enjoy your meal!
