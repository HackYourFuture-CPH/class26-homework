Ingredients

For the outer crust;

    2 cups wheat bulgur,
    250 g ground beef / lamb,
    2 tablespoons semolina,
    1 tablespoon flour,
    2,5 cups water,
    1 tablespoon tomato paste,
    1 tablespoon pepper paste,
    1 egg,
    Salt.

For the filling;

    500 g ground beef / lamb,
    2 onions,
    2/3 cup olive oil,
    1 cup ground walnuts,
    Salt,
    Black pepper.
