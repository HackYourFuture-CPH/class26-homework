Ingredients of Pav Bhaji1 tsp Oil4 cubes large Butter, finely chopped1 cup Onion, chopped1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste1/2 cup Cauliflower, chopped1/2 cup Capsicum, chopped1 cup Potatoes, chopped1/2 cup Peas, chopped2 tsp Salt3 tsp Pav bhaji masala1 tsp Red chilli powder1/2 cup Tomato puree1 cube Butter1 bunch Coriander leavesFor Pav:ButterPavPav bhaji masala

Steps to prepare:
1.Heat oil in a pan. Add cubes of butter along with onion.
2.Saute till golden brown and then add ginger-garlic paste. Mix well.
3.Now add peas along with coriander and mix well, followed by a cup of chopped potatoes.
4.Add chopped cauliflower, salt, red chilli powder and pav bhaji masala to the mashed mixture. Mix well.
5.Now add the tomato puree. Mix the tomato puree thoroughly and then add butter followed by coriander leaves to the cooked bhaaji. Mix thoroughly.

For Pav:
1.Spread some butter all over the pav.
2.Sprinkle pav bhaji masala over it.
3.Toast it on the pan for a while till it becomes golden brown.
4.Serve hot along with a lemon wedge, chopped onion and green chilli.
